components list:
1) Arduino UNO or Nano         1 pice
2) Led                         1 pice
3) Resistor(4.7k or 10k)       1 pice
4) Resistor(330ohm or 1k)      1 pice
5) Push Buttom                 1 pice
6) some jumper wire
7) Breadboard                  1 pice
8) 9-volt battery              1 pice

connection:
Connect the push button between  arduino pin 4  and GND with resistor(4.7k or 10k),and other side connect 5volt.
led pin connect with resistor (330ohm or 1k) in arduino pin 8.led negative pin connect GND.
